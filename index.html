More housing units at risk of wild fire in 2020

Stephen Anti

New York, June, 2022

Millions of homes across the United States are at risk of wild fire as temperatures soar across several states during the summer. According to a 2020 Data from the US Department of Agriculture on Wildfire Risk to Communities, millions of home across the country are exposed to wild fire with California, Texas and Floria among those with the highest risks ( work in progress)
